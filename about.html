Our Story

ClearPath Junk Removal started in 2015 when founder Mike Rodriguez saw his elderly neighbor struggling to clear out years of accumulated items from her garage. After helping her that weekend, Mike realized how many people in our community needed reliable, affordable junk removal services they could trust.

What began with one truck and a commitment to honest work has grown into a full-service junk removal company serving hundreds of homes and businesses across the metro area. But our core values haven't changed: treat every customer with respect, price fairly, and leave every property better than we found it.

Today, we're proud to be a locally owned, family-operated business. Our team consists of background-checked professionals who take pride in their work and genuinely care about helping our neighbors. We're not a franchiseâ€”we're your neighbors, and we're here to stay.
